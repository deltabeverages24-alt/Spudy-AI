About Us

Building Africa's AI Future, One Creation at a Time

Spudy Intelligence is the brainchild of Matipaishe Muduchwa — a passionate tech innovator from Zimbabwe. Spudy (a nickname that embodies creativity and intelligence) was born from a simple belief: Africa deserves world-class AI tools and tech solutions designed with its unique landscape in mind.

We don't just build AI — we build complete digital solutions: from custom websites and mobile apps to AI integration and high-speed connectivity via Starlink. Our team of engineers, designers, and creatives across 12 African countries work tirelessly to empower the next generation of African creators, developers, and entrepreneurs.

From Lagos to Cairo, Nairobi to Cape Town, Spudy is your partner in building without limits.
